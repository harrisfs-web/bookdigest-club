The Elephant & The Rider
🐘
The Elephant — Intuition & Emotion
~90% of the decision
Makes moral judgements instantly, automatically, unconsciously. Steers toward what FEELS right. The real decision-maker.
🧑
The Rider — Reason & Logic
~10% of the decision
Arrives after the elephant has already moved. Finds post-hoc justifications. Thinks it's in charge — but isn't.
↳ Rider serves the Elephant, not the other way around
Moral reasoning is mostly rationalisation — we feel first, then argue.
