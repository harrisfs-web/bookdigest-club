Thiel's 7 Questions Every Business Must Answer
1
⚙️
Engineering
Can you create breakthrough technology, not just incremental improvement?
2
⏰
Timing
Is now the right moment to start this business?
3
🏆

4
👥
People

5
📦

6
⏳

7
