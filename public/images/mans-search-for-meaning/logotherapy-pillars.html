The Three Pillars of Logotherapy
🏛️
Freedom of Will
We always retain the freedom to choose our attitude toward any given circumstance, even in suffering.
🎯
Will to Meaning
The primary human motivation is not pleasure or power, but the search for meaning and purpose.
🌐
Meaning of Life
Life always has meaning, even in suffering and death. Meaning is unique to each person and moment.
