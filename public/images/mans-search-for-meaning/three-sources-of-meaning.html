Three Ways to Find Meaning — Frankl's Framework
🎨
Creative Values — What you GIVE to the world
Work, creation, contribution, building something that outlasts you.
❤️
Experiential Values — What you RECEIVE from the world
Love, beauty, truth, connection with another person.
💪
Attitudinal Values — How you FACE unavoidable suffering
Your stance toward pain you cannot change — the last human freedom.
Even in the worst suffering, at least one source of meaning remains available.
