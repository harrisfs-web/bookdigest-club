8 Scientists Who Rewrote Our Understanding of Everything
⚡
Isaac Newton 1643–1727

Unified gravity, motion, and light into mathematical laws that governed the universe for 200 years

🔬
Lavoisier 1743–1794

Founded modern chemistry by identifying oxygen and establishing the law of conservation of mass

🌋
Charles Lyell 1797–1875

Established deep time: the Earth is billions of years old, not thousands, revolutionising geology and enabling Darwin

🐢
Charles Darwin 1809–1882

Evolution by natural selection explains the origin and diversity of all life on Earth

⚛️
Marie Curie 1867–1934

Discovered radioactivity and polonium; first person to win two Nobel Prizes in two different sciences

🌌
Edwin Hubble 1889–1953

Proved the universe is expanding beyond the Milky Way, establishing the Big Bang framework

🧬
Crick & Franklin Crick 1916–2004 · Franklin 1920–58

Decoded the double-helix structure of DNA, revealing how life copies itself

💥
Georges Lemaître 1894–1966

Proposed the Big Bang theory of the universe's origin; a Belgian priest who changed cosmology
